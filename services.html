Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book my move?

We recommend booking at least 2-4 weeks in advance for local moves and 4-6 weeks for long-distance moves, especially during peak season (May-September).

Do you provide free estimates?

Yes, we offer free, no-obligation estimates for all our moving services. You can request a quote online or schedule an in-home assessment for more accurate pricing.

Are your movers insured?

Absolutely. Steady Moving Services is fully licensed and insured. We offer basic valuation coverage included in your quote, with options for additional coverage available.

What items can't you move?

We cannot transport hazardous materials, perishables, plants, animals, and certain high-value items. Please contact us for a complete list of non-transportable items.

How do you charge for your services?

Local moves are typically charged hourly, while long-distance moves are priced based on distance and shipment weight. Additional services like packing and storage have separate pricing structures.
